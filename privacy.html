Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-09

ARU ("we", "the app") is an internal automation tool that publishes our own original video content to our own connected social accounts.

Information we access

When you connect a social account (e.g. TikTok) to ARU, we access only the authorization token needed to publish videos to that account, which is the account owner's own account. We do not access other users' data, private messages, or personal information.

How we use it

Solely to upload and publish our own video content to our own account via the platform's official Content Posting API. We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.

Data storage

Access tokens are stored securely and used only for publishing. You can revoke ARU's access at any time from your TikTok account settings.
